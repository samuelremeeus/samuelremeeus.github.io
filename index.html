    About

    An ambivert behind
    the lens.

    I'm Samuel Ezra Remeeus, a photographer and videographer based in Bali. I started out at a brand agency in Semarang, working my way up from intern to senior photographer on the strength of my craft.

    In 2022 I went freelance, shooting for companies, brands and agencies across Semarang. Then in 2024, wanting to learn and explore more, I moved to Bali and joined Hype Hunters, a creative podcast and video studio in Kuta, where I spent two years behind the camera. Now I'm leading photo and video at Divino Resort.

    Off set, you'll find me in a deep focus edit with chill jazz on, always chasing something new to learn.
